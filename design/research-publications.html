AutoTrack: A Deep Learning Approach on a Novel Dataset for Vehicle Detection and Route Tracking from CCTV Images

Tracking Accuracy
~83%
IEEE TENSYMP 2025 AI & Computer Vision YOLO-based Models

This research focused on building a deep learning-based pipeline for vehicle detection and route tracking from CCTV images. The work combined model experimentation, license plate recognition, route reconstruction logic, and evaluation on a proposed dataset to better understand multi-frame vehicle tracking challenges.

Key Contributions
Designed and implemented a vehicle detection pipeline using YOLO-based models.
Applied license plate recognition techniques to identify and track vehicles across frames.
Developed a workflow to reconstruct vehicle routes from multiple CCTV inputs.
Achieved approximately 83% tracking accuracy on the proposed dataset.
Contributed to dataset preparation, experimentation, and evaluation.
