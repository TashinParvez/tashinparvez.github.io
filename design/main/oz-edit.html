Background

I’m a Computer Science graduate currently working as a Product Analyst. My interest in product management grew through hands-on projects and through collaborating with PMs and engineers on research, requirement analysis, and product documentation.

My academic background gave me a strong foundation in software engineering, systems thinking, and data. Competitive programming further sharpened how I break down complex problems and think structurally under constraints.

What drives me

What draws me to product management is the opportunity to turn ambiguous user problems into structured solutions. I enjoy understanding behavior, clarifying requirements, and shaping features that balance usability, feasibility, and product goals.

Beyond the work

During university, I actively helped others learn by sharing study materials, problem-solving methods, and practical guidance. That experience strengthened my communication, peer support, and leadership skills.
